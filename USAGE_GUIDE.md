# How to Use

To get the best results from the prompts in this LAB, please follow these guidelines:

### Placeholders
Many prompts contain variables in square brackets, for example: `[insert topic]` or `[target audience]`.

**Action:**
1. Copy the prompt.
2. Replace everything inside and including the `[]` brackets with your own specific data.
3. Paste the modified prompt into your AI.

### Language
All prompts are designed for the **English** language to ensure maximum performance and logic consistency across all global AI models.
